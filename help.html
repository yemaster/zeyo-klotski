Zeyo Klotski

What's Klotski?

This game is called Digital Klotski. Digital Klotski is to use as few steps as possible, as short as possible time, the number of squares on the board, according to the order from left to right, from top to bottom to rearrange neatly.

How can I play the game?

There're two modes, single player and double players. Enter the game, you can use WASD or the arrow key to control the numbers(In single mode, both are OK while multiplayer mode control one side).
